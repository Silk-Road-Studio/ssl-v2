What is Sustainable Sri Lanka?

A sustainable tourism consultancy specialising in helping organisations improve their enviromental, economic and social impact in Sri Lanka.

Internationally-recognized sustainability certification is well on its way to becoming a mandatory requirement for travel and tourism businesses both in Sri Lanka and abroad. Foreign tour operators and travel agents expect you to be certified or working towards certification in order to prioritise your products and services. Consumers are effectively demanding sustainable alternatives when selecting accomodation and tour companies. As a trusted advisor Sustainable Sri Lanka will help your team work towards certification and prepare the organization for the official audits.

Climate risks are a larger part of your corporate responsibility and risk management discussions. Mitigating climate risk by reducing your impact on the environment begins with assessing your environmental practices and benchmarking them against standards.

Sustainable Sri Lanka will help you set out a framework that you company can follow so that it can provide assurance to company management and employees as well as external stakeholders that environmental impact is being measured and improved.

If you want to be a responsible member of the community you need to maintain integrity, fairness and honesty in all your internal and external relationships.

We will help your business find suitable projects and formulate and implement a sustainable community engagement policy and practices, so they contribute positively to the social and economic development of regions in which they operate.

Does your team understand sustainability? Are they familiar with responsible travel? Does the organization know what its doing right and what it may be doing wrong or not enough of?

At Sustainable Sri Lanka we can assist you with staff training on sustainability covering areas from hazardous materials to child protection, from energy saving to community engagement. With multi-lingual options we can provide your staff with the training they need.

When designing experiences it is important that they ensure to the protection, preservation and enhancement of local sites and traditions of historical, archaeological, cultural and spiritual significance. Do you ensure that all the experiences you offer your clients have a positive impact?

Sustainable Sri Lanka is here to help your organization create and operate experiences consistent with principles of sustainability.

Sustainable organizations are continually setting goals and targets that minimise negative impacts and increase positive ones.

>We will help you report details the progress you have made against the targets you set. This will include performance in this reporting period compared to a previous time period. It will also include benchmarking against.

Targets may include carbon emissions reduction, energy and water reduction, renewable energy usage, socially-driven objectives. We will also help you credibly communicate your approach and achievements.

Operating your business in a socially and environmentally friendly manner not only makes good business sense, its no longer an option in today's world.
